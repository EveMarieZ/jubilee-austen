Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean and elegant code.

A little bit about me

My journey into tech started when I moved to London to pursue a master's degree in design history. During the course I studied alongside artists and designers at both the Royal College of Art and Victoria and Albert Museum and wanted to understand their practice by undertaking it myself. I decided to create a dynamic online space for my writing from scratch.

After diving into coding, I never looked back. Coding gave me the ability to make my writing shine, and now I want to focus on creating spaces for others to shine as well. I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
